The Bursary Accountability Gap

👤

3.2 million learners affected

Delayed and stolen bursaries push low-income students out of school every term, creating high dropout rates.

💵

Ksh 12 billion lost annually

Ghost students and non-existent schools siphon funds that were meant for real children from disadvantaged families.

📍

290 constituencies, no unified tracking

Every constituency handles bursaries differently. Parents have no single centralized place to verify disbursements.
